More About Ministry of Foreign Affairs

This is detailed information about the types of passport. You can apply for the type that fit your category. The types or categories of passports are: Ordinary Passport, Service Passport, Official Passports and Diplomatic Passports.





The Liberia Passport Office under the Ministry of Foreign Affairs is authorized to issue Liberian Passports. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs by this mandate is providing applicants with this online passport application service






Passport Renewal Service now available to applicants residing in territories that the Government of Liberia has no passport processing center. Applicants must be 18 years and above at the time of his/her last in-person application. You can access the Passport Renewal Service here
